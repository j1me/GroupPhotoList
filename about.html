FaceFrame

About FaceFrame

FaceFrame is a privacy-first web application for detecting and extracting faces from group photos. What makes it special is that all processing happens right in your browser - your photos never leave your device.

Privacy First

Unlike traditional face detection services that require uploading your photos to a server, FaceFrame processes everything locally using your browser's capabilities:

  • No server uploads - images stay on your device
  • Processing happens entirely in your browser
  • No data collection or tracking
  • Open source and transparent

How It Works

FaceFrame uses face-api.js, a powerful face detection library that runs entirely in the browser. When you upload a photo, it's processed locally using your device's resources, ensuring your privacy while still providing accurate face detection.
